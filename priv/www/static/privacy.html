Twitcoin Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how and when we collect and use your information when you use Twitcoin.

Information Collection and Use

Personal Identification Information

The sources that we use to collect information about you are the Twitcoin web site and the Twitter API. Generally, we do not collect any information about you that is not publicly available in the first place. The only information that we use as your user profile is your personal Bitcoin wallet data, which we keep private and safely encrypted.

Non-Personal Identification Information

We collect non-personal identification information whenever you interact with our web site. More precisely, that is your IP address, as well as the name and type of your browser. Furthermore, we use so-called cookies in order to authenticate you when you have signed in to our web site.

If you have any questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@twitcoin.net .
