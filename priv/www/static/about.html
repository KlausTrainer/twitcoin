About Twitcoin

Twitcoin is an online wallet application that associates your Twitter account to a personal online wallet storing bitcoins (which we simply call "wallet" from now on here). By crediting or rather debiting wallets, Twitcoin enables Twitter account owners to both receive bitcoins from and send bitcoins to any Twitter account or Bitcoin address.

Twitcoin provides a "Sign in with twitter"-functionality in order to give you access to your wallet. Wallets are automatically created by the Twitcoin application as necessary. That is, a wallet is created when someone signs in for the first time, or when someone sends bitcoins to a Twitter account that has not got a wallet associated to it yet. Wallets have Bitcoin addresses and can be credited by sending bitcoins to their respective address.

We use Twitter solely as an identity provider to manage access to wallets. That is, your Twitter account credentials are only used for authorizing access to your wallet, but not, for instance, to access your timeline or direct messages.
